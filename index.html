General Information

JOYORA’26 is an inter-college symposium organized by the Business Department of Joy University. The event provides a professional platform for students to showcase creativity, leadership, communication, and managerial skills.

Rules & Regulations (Applicable to All Events)

  • Online registration is mandatory for all participants.
  • Participants must carry a valid college ID card.
  • Reporting time must be strictly followed.
  • No misbehavior or misconduct will be tolerated.
  • Organizers reserve the right to modify rules if required.
  • Judges’ and organizers’ decisions will be final and binding.
  • Participation of an individual is restricted to only one event.
